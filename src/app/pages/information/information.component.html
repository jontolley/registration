Merit Badge Information and Prerequisites

Schedules for each of the merit badges will differ depending difficulty and the approaches of each of the counselors. During the first activity session of the encampment (Monday from 2pm-5pm) Young Men should visit with the counselors they want to take merit badges from, and determine the best way to fit them into their week.

Prerequisites
  • Aviation: requirement #5
  • Communication: requirements #5 and #9
  • Fishing: please catch a fish before hand and bring a picture of it. Also, cook a fish that you caught or cook one purchased from a store.
  • Pioneering: requirements #5 and 10 can be done ahead of time.
  • Photography: bring your own camera.
  • Pulp and Paper: requirement #6. A mandatory field trip on Wednesday morning. No shorts or sandals will be allowed.
  • Widerness Survival: overnight shelter experience will have to be done before or after encampment.
  • Wood Carving: Bring a valid Totin chip card.
